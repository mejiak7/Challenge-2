About Me


Hello, my name is Katherine and I am 28 years old. I was born and raised in the Bronx, NY. I had the oportunity to see the mechanics of how each element of the device works. Now, I'm ready to learn how it works behind the scenes. I'm excited to grow and become a full web developer and be part of lots of exciting projects.
